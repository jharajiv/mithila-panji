## Note on the underlying tradition

The Panji Prabandh corpus is a collective cultural heritage of the Maithil
community, sustained over seven centuries by hereditary Panjikars. The
specific *transcription* and *digital structuring* released here is what
this licence covers; the *tradition itself* belongs to the community and
cannot be owned by any single party.
