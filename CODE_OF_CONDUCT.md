# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Mithila-Panji project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, caste, sub-
community, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- The use of sexualised language or imagery.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political
  attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information — including, for this project,
  unconsented disclosure of living individuals' genealogical details —
  without explicit permission.
- Caste-based or community-based hostility, even when framed as humour.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of
acceptable behaviour and are expected to take appropriate and fair
corrective action in response to any behaviour they deem inappropriate,
threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues and
pull requests, mailing lists, chat channels, and in-person events held in
the project's name.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may
be reported to **rajiv.jha@myvyoma.io**. All complaints will be reviewed
and investigated promptly and confidentially.
